Backgammon Instructions

Dive into the classic game of Backgammon and sharpen your strategic skills!

Objective:

The goal is to move all of your pieces onto your home board and then bear them off before your adversary does.

Setup:

Both players commence with 15 checkers positioned on the board as shown:

Gameplay:

Players alternately roll the dice and shift their checkers in accordance to the numbers shown on the dice.

Moving Checkers:

Select a checker by clicking on it, then choose a permissible point to transfer it. The board's points range from 1 to 24, initiating from your home board.

Hitting and Bearing Off:

When your checker lands on a spot occupied by a single opponent's checker, that checker is "hit" and positioned on the bar. For bearing off, transition your checkers onto your home board, then move them off based on the dice outcomes.

Winning:

Emerging victorious is a matter of being the first to bear off all of your checkers from the board.
